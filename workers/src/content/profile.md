# Francesco Foresta

## About

Software architect with twelve years of experience spanning cloud-native telecom, SDN/NFV research, and production-grade system architecture for 5G RAN. The professional arc begins in academic research on OpenStack and SDN controller integration at Università di Bologna, where four peer-reviewed publications and an IEEE Best Paper Award established a foundation in cloud infrastructure design. A transition through production network engineering at Laboratori Guglielmo Marconi led into JMA Wireless, where eight years of progressively broader ownership covered cloud-native baseband execution, O-RAN management and timing architecture, containerization platform strategy, vendor interoperability, standards engagement, and RAN & DAS system architecture.

The technical signature is a preference for architectural realism over tooling enthusiasm — understanding where cloud-native abstractions serve telecom workloads and where they do not, and making that distinction concrete through hands-on validation, benchmarking, and vendor integration rather than paper analysis. Work has consistently combined system-level architecture with direct implementation involvement, from Kubernetes cluster configuration and PTP timing validation to NETCONF/YANG requirements definition and multi-vendor O-RU integration.

Current scope includes 5G system architecture, O-RAN and 3GPP standards strategy, cloud-native platform assessment, DAS/RAN convergence architecture, and research collaboration with universities.
